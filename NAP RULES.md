# NAP RULES 2407
## 2407 Non-Aggression Pact (NAP) Guidelines
Effective Date: Two days prior to the start of the next SvS Prep Phase (4-week cycle)
Contact for Questions: WTF, TNT, KOR, or FUN Leadership

## I. NAP Membership & Structure
### 1. Eligibility & Selection
- The top 7 strongest alliances based on power will be eligible for NAP membership, confirmed every Saturday before SvS Prep begins.
- Participation is voluntary, and alliances may choose to accept or decline.

### 2. Commitment Duration
- Once accepted, alliances must remain in the NAP for the full 4-week cycle.
- No early exits are allowed.

### 3. Academy Alliances
- Official academy alliances must be clearly labeled (e.g., “WTF-Academy”).
- Academies are under full NAP protection and must follow all NAP rules.
- Hostile actions against an academy are considered NAP violations.

## II. Behavior & Conduct Expectations
### 4. Mutual Respect & Non-Interference
No Poaching:
- NAP members may not recruit or solicit players from other NAP alliances.
- Transfers must be voluntary and without solicitation.
City & Tile Protection:
- NAP members may not attack each other’s cities or gathering tiles.
- Exception: Gathering tile hits are permitted during Kill Events (KE) only.
Banner Burning Restrictions:
- Burning another alliance’s banner requires prior approval and a 24-hour response window.
- Unauthorized burning is a violation and subject to leadership review.

### 5. Event Participation Boundaries
- All NAP members must participate in statewide events within their home alliance only.
- Cross-alliance participation is prohibited. 

### 6. Alliance Loyalty During Battles
- Members may only fight for facilities, forts, or strongholds (SHs) within their home alliance.
- Assisting other alliances (including academies or former alliances) is not allowed.

## III. Forts & Strongholds
### 7. Assignment & Participation Guidelines
- Fort/SH assignments are based on SvS Prep Phase contributions, not total alliance power alone.
- Members must announce chosen forts/SHs before sign-ups to avoid overlap.
- Remaining forts will be listed in World Chat for non-NAP alliances.
- Battle Rules:
- No scouting or city attacks during fort/SH battles.
- First hit on an unoccupied fort/SH must be a rally.
- Non-NAP alliances may participate freely, but attacks on cities will be met with retaliation.

### 8. Transfers & Courtesy
- Forts and SHs may be gifted or transferred to any alliance, NAP or non-NAP.
- All NAP members are expected to communicate clearly and respectfully around assignments and transfers.

### 9. Lowest-Contributing Alliance Rule
- The NAP alliance ranks 7th or lower in SvS Prep Phase contributions will not be eligible to receive or compete for any forts or SHs for that cycle.
- However, if the 6th and 7th ranked NAP alliance still places within the top 12 alliances globally (including both states), each may be awarded one fort.

## IV. SvS Participation & Solar Castle
### 10. Combat Participation Requirements
- All NAP alliances must actively participate in SvS battles.
- NAP alliances are expected to support all participating alliances in reaching Level 4 Combat Engineering.

### 11. Solar Castle Presidency
- The Solar Castle will rotate among NAP alliances.
- Candidates must be approved by majority vote of current NAP members.
- Academy alliances are not eligible to hold or vote in the rotation.

## V. Enforcement & Violations
### 12. Reporting & Accountability
- All rule violations must be reported to NAP leadership.
- Any hostile action (e.g., attacks, scouting, unauthorized banner burns) without leadership consultation is considered a violation.
- Repeated or severe offenses may result in removal from the NAP.
